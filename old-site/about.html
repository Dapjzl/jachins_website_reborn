About JACHINS

Across Asset Lifecycle & Across Africa

JACHINS Development Limited is equipped to provide value-adding services across various terrains and operating environments in the oil and gas sector. With over 15 years of proven expertise, we specialize in the sustainable development of people, assets, and critical infrastructure.

At the forefront of innovation, we utilize cutting-edge technology to deliver solutions that surpass industry standards — backed by world-class research, meticulous planning, and data-driven insights.

"Let's propel you to the zenith of success!"

Our Core Services

Three Pillars of Development

People

We invest in human capital through strategic consultancy, talent development, and workforce planning — building the competent teams that power Africa's energy industry.

Assets

From conception to decommissioning, we manage energy assets with precision — ensuring maximum value, safety, and efficiency at every stage of the asset lifecycle.

Critical Infrastructure

We design, build, and maintain the pipelines, facilities, and processing systems that form the backbone of Nigeria's oil and gas sector.
