Field Development Strategy

We convert subsurface and reservoir insights into executable field development strategies, optimizing well placement, facilities sizing, and phased investment plans while delivering production forecasts and development concept evaluations.

Explore

Feasibility & Concept Selection

We evaluate early-stage development concepts against technical, cost, and schedule risks to support concept selection, delivering cost estimates, option ranking, preliminary HSE screening, and constructability recommendations before FEED approval.

Explore

Petroleum Economics

We develop and stress-test economic models for E&P assets, evaluating NPV, IRR, payback, and fiscal sensitivities to support investment decisions, partner approvals, and reserves-to-revenue planning.

Explore

Engineering Design

We deliver multi-disciplinary engineering from Pre-FEED to detailed design, producing process, piping, structural, electrical, and instrumentation packages, while supporting HAZOP, SIL/LOPA studies, and constructability readiness for EPC execution.

Explore

Procurement, Construction & Installation

We oversee vendor selection, procurement, fabrication supervision, and construction execution for pipelines and facilities, ensuring QA/QC compliance, mechanical completion, and readiness through start-up preparation.

Explore

General Industry

We deliver EPC services across energy, power, water, and industrial sectors, executing civil, piping, structural, and electrical works under consistent QHSE standards and disciplined project delivery practices.
