About JACHINS

Across Asset Lifecycle &
Across Africa

JACHINS Development Limited is equipped to provide value-adding services across various terrains and operating environments in the oil and gas sector. With over 15 years of proven expertise, we specialize in the sustainable development of people, assets, and critical infrastructure.

At the forefront of innovation, we utilize cutting edge technology to deliver solutions that surpass industry standards backed by world-class research, meticulous planning, and data-driven insights.

Everything you need to know about our services, safety standards, and operational capabilities.

JACHINS adheres to OSHA regulations, API standards, ISO certifications (9001, 14001, 45001), and comprehensive Health, Safety, and Environmental (HSE) guidelines across all operations. Zero-LTI culture is embedded across every project team.

Through advanced project management techniques, automation and digital technologies, a highly skilled workforce, continuous training programs, and supply chain optimisation all governed by disciplined QHSE practices.

JACHINS' three service pillars are: People (consultancy & workforce development via JETS), Assets (lifecycle management & integrity), and Critical Infrastructure (engineering, procurement & construction delivery).
